What's the refund policy? Refunds are only eligible after an investigation of a faulty product. Refunds are not eligible on orders that have been marked as delivered by carrier. Customers may also request a refund before the order has shipped out. If a product was returned to the sender due to a wrong address a refund may be given for the products, NOT the shipping fee.

How does shipping work? Shipping rates are automated on the website based on weight. All orders are shipped with USPS, as a small business it is the most affordable. Therefore, it is the default option on our website. If you would like to request another shipping carrier please let me know before your purchase. Pricing may differ based on the carrier you choose.

Can I pick up my order? Pickup/Meetup is avaliable, after processing, your order will be marked as ready and you will be notified using contact information from the order or Instagram DM. Pickup or meetup must be scheduled within 5 days of being ready or it will be canceled and restocked. Pickups/meetups have to be confirmed the day before your scheduled window.
